Linux networking commands

ip (internet protocol)
e.g: ip a

ping
e.g: ping google.com

curl
e.g: curl https://sufiyan-khan-portfolio.lovable.app/

wget webget
e.g: wget https://example.com/file.txt

ss socket statistics
e.g: ss -tulnp 
flags: 
t > TCP
u > UDP
l > listening
u > numbers
p > process names
